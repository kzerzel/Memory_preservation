How It Works

  1. 1. Create an Account – invite the person you wish to preserve or upload existing recordings.
  2. 2. Capture Memories – record guided interviews and upload photos, videos, letters, voice notes.
  3. 3. AI Training – EU‑hosted engines build a voice clone, animate images, and learn the storytelling style.
  4. 4. Interact Forever – family can chat, hear new messages, or schedule future greetings.
