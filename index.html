How It Works

1 — Create an Account

Sign up (free) with your email and choose who can view your memories.

2 — Capture Memories

Record audio stories, upload photos & videos, or write reflections with our guided prompts.

3 — Curate & Share

Organise memories into collections, set privacy rules, and invite family or friends to explore them.

4 — Revisit Anytime

Search, filter, and (optionally) chat with an AI voice that brings those memories to life—today, tomorrow, or fifty years from now.
