How It Works

  1. Create an Account → invite the person to preserve or upload existing recordings.
  2. Capture Memories → record guided interviews; upload photos, videos, voice notes.
  3. AI Training → EU‑hosted engines build a voice clone, animate images, and learn the storytelling style.
  4. Interact Forever → family can chat, hear new messages, or schedule future greetings.
